The Art of Baklava

A dessert with a rich history, baklava is a symbol of celebration and craftsmanship. Its origins trace back to the Ottoman Empire, where it was perfected in the imperial kitchens. At Tulumbak, we honor this legacy by meticulously layering 40 sheets of paper-thin phyllo dough with the finest pistachios and walnuts, then bathing it in a delicate, house-made syrup for a taste that is both timeless and unforgettable.
